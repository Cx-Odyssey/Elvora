Our Story
✦ Why ELVORA Exists
Beauty Beyond Limits
ELVORA began with a simple frustration: finding genuinely authentic skincare in Bangladesh shouldn't be this hard.
Too many shoppers in Dhaka have been burned by counterfeit products sold as the real thing — products that don't work, or worse, irritate the skin that trusted them. We started ELVORA, based in Savar, Dhaka, to be the shop we wished existed: one where every single product on the shelf is verified authentic, sourced properly, and where you can walk in, ask a real question, and get a real answer.

We collect only what's needed to process your order: your name, phone number, and delivery address at checkout. If you create an account, we also store your email. None of this is ever sold or shared with third parties — it's used solely to fulfil your order and contact you about it.

Returns & Refunds
Unopened, unused items in their original packaging can be returned within 7 days of delivery. To start a return, message us on WhatsApp with your Order ID and the reason — we'll guide you through the next steps.
For hygiene reasons, opened skincare and beauty items cannot be returned unless the product itself is faulty or damaged on arrival — in that case, contact us within 48 hours with a photo and we'll make it right.
Approved refunds are issued via bank transfer or store credit, whichever you prefer.

FAQ
Is everything on ELVORA genuinely authentic?
Yes — every product we sell is 100% authentic. We don't source from unverified resellers, and we're happy to show proof of sourcing if you ever want to ask.
Do I need an account to order?
No — you can check out as a guest with just your name, phone, and address. Creating an account lets you track order history, save a wishlist, and write reviews.
How much is delivery, and how long does it take?
৳80 within Dhaka, ৳150 outside Dhaka. Orders typically arrive within 1–3 business days. Free delivery appears automatically during select promotions — check the homepage banner.
What payment methods do you accept?
Cash on Delivery right now — pay when your order arrives. bKash is coming soon.
How do I track my order?
Every order confirmation includes a "Track via WhatsApp" button. Signed-in customers can also see live status under Account → My Orders.
Can I cancel an order?
Yes, as long as it hasn't shipped yet. Open the order under Account → My Orders and tap Cancel.
What's your return policy?
Unopened items in original packaging can be returned within 7 days. Opened items are only eligible if the product arrived damaged or faulty. Full details are on our Policies page.
Do you have a physical store?
Yes — Savar, Dhaka. You're welcome to visit, test products in person, and shop directly. See Our Store for address and hours.
How do promo codes work?
Enter your code in the Promo Code box at checkout — the discount applies instantly if it's valid. Codes may have a minimum order amount or expiry date.
